BINI

BINI is a rising Filipino girl group whose name comes from the word binibini, meaning "young lady." The group is composed of talented members: Aiah, Colet, Gwen, Jhoanna, Maloi, Mikha, Sheena, and Stacey. Despite their diverse personalities, origins, and beliefs, they come together in perfect harmony, united by their passion for music and their love for one another. BINI continues to inspire audiences through their synchronized artistry and unwavering dedication, proving that their love for music and each other is their greatest strength.

Articles

  • Grand BINIverse Concert: BINI's "Grand BINIverse" concert will take place from November 16 to 18, 2024, at the Smart Araneta Coliseum. Special guest performances include OPM legends like Regine Velasquez-Alcasid and Gary Valenciano, alongside other artists such as Vice Ganda and Maymay Entrata. The event has sold out, but livestream tickets are still available.
  • Award Recognition: BINI won the "Best Asia Act" at the 2024 MTV Europe Music Awards (EMA), beating artists like ILLIT from South Korea. This win emphasizes BINI's growing influence in the global music scene.
  • Music Releases: BINI is releasing their albums "Born To Win," "Feel Good," and the EP "Talaarawan" on vinyl. Fans can also enjoy their first-ever holiday cover of "Joy To The World."
  • BINI’s "BINIverse" Concert Preparation: BINI's live band is gearing up for the three-day "BINIverse" concert, which will feature their hit songs and new arrangements.
  • Christmas Music Releases: BINI has released a Christmas remix of "Cherry On Top" alongside their cover of "Joy To The World." The animated music video highlights Filipino holiday traditions.
